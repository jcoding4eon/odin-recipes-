Chicken Al Pastor

Description

If you are looking for the best chicken al pastor recipe, you've come to the right place. Here is everything you need to know about making the best chicken al pastor of your life.

Ingredients

  • 4 dried gudajilo chiles, stemmed and seeded
  • 2 tablespoons achiote paste
  • 6 large cloves garlics, peeled
  • 1 (7 ounce) can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, drained
  • 1 1/3 cups finely chopped onion, divided
  • 2/3 cup organce juice
  • 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 1 teasppon kosher salt
  • 3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut in half
  • 8 (12-inch) jumbo wooden skewers
  • 1 large fresh pineapple-peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 20 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup roughly chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high head.
  2. Combine ingredients in a blender.
  3. Refridgerate marinade at least 8 hours.
  4. Place wooden skewers in a shallow dish.
  5. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C.
  6. Thread pineapple, pierce through the center core and brush with remaining marinade.
  7. Placed stacked skewers in the prepared pan.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  9. Heat a large skillet over high heat
  10. Remove chicken and pineapple from skewers.
  11. Divide chicken and pineapple between heated tortillas.
