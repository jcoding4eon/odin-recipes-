French Toast Recipe

Description

If you are looking for the best French toast recipe, you've come to the right place. Here is everything you need to know about making the best French toast of your life.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vaniila extract (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 table spoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Add More Recipes

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot.
